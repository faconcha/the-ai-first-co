# Alex Hormozi - Marketing & Offers Principles

## Value Equation
The perceived value of any offer is determined by four variables:
- Dream Outcome: What the customer truly wants (maximize)
- Perceived Likelihood of Achievement: How likely they believe they'll get it (maximize)
- Time Delay: How long before they see results (minimize)
- Effort & Sacrifice: What they have to give up or do (minimize)

Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)

## Grand Slam Offer Framework
1. Identify the dream outcome your customer wants
2. List every obstacle between them and that outcome
3. For each obstacle, create a solution
4. Bundle solutions into a single, irresistible offer
5. Enhance with scarcity (limited quantity), urgency (limited time), bonuses, and guarantees

## Lead Magnet Principles
- A lead magnet should solve a specific, narrow problem completely
- It should demonstrate your expertise by giving real value upfront
- The best lead magnets create a need for the next thing you sell
- Format: checklists, templates, calculators, mini-courses, free trials
- Must be consumed quickly (low effort) and deliver fast results (low time delay)

## Content & Advertising Rules
- Lead with value, not with pitch
- The best ads don't look like ads - they look like content
- Hook → Retain → Reward: every piece of content needs all three
- Specificity sells: "lose 10 lbs in 30 days" beats "lose weight fast"
- Use proof: numbers, testimonials, case studies, before/after
- Volume wins: more content = more at-bats = more conversions

## Pricing & Positioning
- Price on value, not on cost
- Higher prices attract better customers and create better outcomes
- The goal is to make the price trivial relative to the value delivered
- Use anchoring: show the full value before revealing the price

## Growth Levers
1. Get more customers (lead generation)
2. Increase average transaction value (upsells, cross-sells)
3. Increase purchase frequency (retention, subscriptions)
4. Improve conversion rate at every step of the funnel
